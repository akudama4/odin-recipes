Pizza Recipe

How to make pizza?

This recipe will show you how to make the most delicous pizza in the world

Ingredients

  • Yeast
  • Sugar
  • Flour
  • Olive Oil
  • Tomatoo sauce
  • Mozarella Cheese
  • Pepperoni

Steps

  1. Make a dough
  2. Let it rise for a bit
  3. Form a pizza shape
  4. Add Tomatoo sauce, mozarella cheese and sliced pepperoni
  5. Bake in oven, 240 degrees C for 10 minutes
