Chicken with Rice

About

This dish is perfect choice for anybody that wants to keep his protien up to date or maybe is taking care of his weight. Simple, healthy, fast and tasty!

Ingredients

  • 200g of chicken
  • 100g of rice
  • seasoning of choice
  • a little bit of olive oil
  • Vegatebales of choice (not mandatory)

Steps

  1. Slice the chicken into cubes.
  2. Season the chicken and let it rest for 10 minutes.
  3. Start cooking rice.
  4. Heaten the pan with a little bit of olive oil.
  5. Put chicken on pan and start frying.
  6. After chicken get some colour you can add vegs if you willing to.
  7. When rice is ready, mix it with chicken and vegs or pan.
  8. Enjoy your meal!
