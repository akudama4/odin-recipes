Pizza witch chicken

About

The best pizza in the world. Ideal dish if you willing to combine wannabe cheatday with some solid protein intake. Lets go!

Ingredients

  • 200g of cooked chicken
  • 200g of light mozarella cheese
  • pizza dough of choice
  • tomatoe sauce
  • Vegatebales of choice

Steps

  1. Spray a little olive oil on over-pan.
  2. Spread the pizza dough over oven-pan.
  3. Add tomatoe sauce first.
  4. Add chesse.
  5. Add chicken and Vegatebales.
  6. Put in into heathen oven (240 celcuis degree) for 11 minutes.
  7. Take it out and enjoy your pizza!
