Peanut Butter & Jelly Recipe

About

A classic sandwich that everybody loves. This sandwich contains peanut butter and jelly. Awesome choce for breakfast or quick afternoon snack.

Ingredients

  • 2 or 4 slices of bread
  • Peanut butter of choice
  • Jelly of choice

Steps

  1. Put silces of bread on the plate.
  2. Spread the peanut buttor on first slice.
  3. Spread some jelly on the second slice.
  4. Put both slices together and enjoy your sandwich!
